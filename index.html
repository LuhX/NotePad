NoNonsense Notes is a app where you can save short notes or tasks. It tries to be as simple as possible without sacrificing functionality.

It keeps your stuff safe in the cloud by syncing with Google Tasks. This way, your notes are always up to date across all your devices.

You can switch between different themes and looks. The settings offers a whole range of customization. Don't care for the check boxes in the list? Hide them!

Perhaps the most important thing of all is that the app is free and open source. Download the installer straight from GitHub, or get it from Google Play.
